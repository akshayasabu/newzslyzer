╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

  Step 1: User reads articles
          ↓
  Step 2: System tracks categories automatically
          ↓
  Step 3: Preferences calculated (e.g., Sports: 5, Tech: 3)

  Step 4: User logs out and logs back in
          ↓
